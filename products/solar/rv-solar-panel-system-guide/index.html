Pro Tips from Experienced Bloggers

01

Prefer large single high-wattage panels (400-700W) over many small ones.

02

Always use tiltable or pull-out brackets – increases generation by 25-35%.

03

Match solar power with battery: 1000W solar needs at least 30kWh battery.

04

For southern rainy areas, go for 1500W. Northwest highlands do well with 1200W.
