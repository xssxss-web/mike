Door Lock Manufacturing and Global Supply Solutions

Door locks play a critical role in residential, commercial and industrial security systems. China has become one of the world's largest manufacturing centers for door locks and architectural hardware. Manufacturers produce a wide range of products including mortise locks, deadbolt locks, lever handle locks, commercial door locks, security locks and custom OEM solutions for global markets.

Types of Door Locks Available

Modern door lock systems are designed for different applications and security requirements. Mortise locks remain one of the most popular choices for commercial buildings and hotels due to their durability and high-security performance. Deadbolt locks are widely used in residential properties, while commercial lever handle locks provide convenience and compliance with accessibility requirements.

Security door locks are increasingly used in high-risk environments where enhanced protection is required. Many international buyers also seek customized lock systems with specific cylinder technologies, finishes and branding options.

Why International Buyers Source Door Locks From China

China offers a complete manufacturing ecosystem for architectural hardware. Buyers can access suppliers specializing in lock bodies, cylinders, handles, keys, finishing processes and packaging solutions within the same supply chain network.

The availability of OEM and ODM services allows brands, wholesalers and distributors to develop customized products while maintaining competitive costs. Combined with efficient logistics infrastructure, China continues to be a preferred sourcing destination for global buyers.
