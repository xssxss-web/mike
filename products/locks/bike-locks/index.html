Bike Lock Security Levels Guide

Choosing the right security level helps protect bicycles, e-bikes and personal mobility devices against theft.

Level 1

Basic Security

Suitable for children's bikes, low-risk areas and short-term parking. Cable locks are commonly used at this level.

Level 2

Medium Security

Recommended for daily commuting and moderate-risk environments. Folding locks offer a good balance between convenience and protection.

Level 3

High Security

Designed for city commuting and expensive bicycles. U-locks provide strong resistance against cutting and leverage attacks.

Level 4

Maximum Security

Ideal for premium bicycles, e-bikes and high-theft-risk locations. Heavy-duty chain locks and hardened U-lock systems provide the highest level of protection.

Popular Types of Bike Locks

U-locks remain one of the most trusted solutions for protecting bicycles in urban environments. Their hardened steel construction offers strong resistance against cutting and leverage attacks. Chain locks provide flexibility and are commonly used for bicycles, e-bikes and motorcycles requiring versatile locking options.

Folding locks have become increasingly popular due to their compact design and easy portability. Cable locks remain a lightweight solution suitable for short-term parking and lower-risk applications.
