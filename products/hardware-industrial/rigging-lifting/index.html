Frequently Asked Questions

What is rigging hardware?

Rigging hardware refers to components used to securely lift, hoist, and move heavy loads, including shackles, hooks, turnbuckles, wire rope clips, and slings.

What are the main types of shackles?

The most common types are Bow Shackles (Omega), D Shackles (Dee), Anchor Shackles, Screw Pin and Bolt Type shackles.

What is the difference between bow shackles and D shackles?

Bow shackles have a large rounded body suitable for multi-directional loads and multiple slings. D shackles feature narrow straight sides for vertical single-point lifting with higher compact load capacity.

How do I choose the correct turnbuckle?

Consider working load limit (WLL), end fittings (Eye-Eye, Hook-Hook, Jaw-Jaw), material (galvanized or stainless steel), and application environment.
