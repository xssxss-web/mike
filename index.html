Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Yiwu Sourcing Agent and why should I choose one in Yiwu?

A Yiwu Sourcing Agent helps overseas buyers find, negotiate, inspect and ship products from Yiwu International Trade City — the world's largest small commodity wholesale market. Being based directly in Yiwu allows me to offer lower MOQ, faster response, and better prices than general China sourcing agents.

What is your service fee? How do you make money?

I charge a transparent service fee of 1%-3% of the total order value. You pay the factory price directly. There is no hidden markup. This model keeps prices competitive and transparent for small and medium businesses.

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ) in Yiwu?

Most products can start from 10-100 pieces per style. This is one of the biggest advantages of sourcing in Yiwu compared to other Chinese manufacturing cities. I can also help mix different styles to meet your MOQ.

Do you provide quality inspection and photos?

Yes. I personally inspect every order and send you detailed photos and videos before shipment. This is included in my service to ensure product quality meets your expectations.

Which countries do you ship to?

I ship worldwide, with the majority of clients coming from the USA, Europe, Australia, Canada, and the Middle East. I can help arrange DDP, DDU, or FOB shipping according to your needs.

How long does shipping take from Yiwu to the USA or Europe?

By sea: 25-40 days. By air: 7-12 days. Express (DHL/UPS): 5-9 days. I will recommend the most cost-effective solution based on your order size and urgency.

Can you help with custom packaging and branding?

Yes. I can arrange custom packaging, private labeling, logo printing, and even simple product customization for many items.

Are you a real person based in Yiwu or just an online agent?

I am a real one-man SOHO based inside Yiwu International Trade City. You can visit my office when you come to Yiwu. I walk the market daily to source products for my clients.

How do I start working with you?

Just send me your product requirements (pictures, specifications, target price, etc.) via WhatsApp or email. I will reply with quotations and available options within 4 hours.

Do you accept small trial orders?

Yes. I specialize in helping small and medium businesses. Many clients start with a small test order of $500–$2000 before placing larger orders.
